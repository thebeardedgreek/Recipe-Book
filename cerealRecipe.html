Cereal Recipe!

Country of Origin: United States (New York)

Ingredients:

  • 1 serving of your favorite cereal
  • 1 bowl
  • 1 Spoon
  • 3 - 5 cups of milk

Directions:

Open the container of cereal and take the bowl. Pour cereal into the bowl until it is about half to 2/3rds filled.

Pour in milk into bowl until cereal raises to fill rest of bowl.

Take spoon, put in bowl.

Serves 1 person
